Privacy

Privacy notes

隐私说明

TipTap is built as a local-first macOS input utility. This page explains the product intent in plain language.

TipTap 是一个本地优先的 macOS 输入工具。本页用直接的语言说明它的隐私设计。

Local use

本地使用

TipTap does not require an account and does not include product analytics or telemetry. Selection actions, gesture bindings, and clipboard history are designed to run on your Mac.

TipTap 不需要账号，也不包含产品分析或遥测。选区动作、手势绑定和剪贴板历史都设计为在你的 Mac 上运行。

Third-party destinations

第三方目标

Some actions, such as Search, Translate, Open Link, Open With Browser, or Run Shortcut, can send selected content to the destination you choose. Those destinations are governed by their own privacy policies.

搜索、翻译、打开链接、选择浏览器打开或运行快捷指令等动作，可能会把选中内容发送到你选择的目标。那些目标适用各自的隐私政策。

Excluded apps

排除的 App

TipTap includes exclusions so you can keep the selection popup away from sensitive or high-focus apps. Advanced gestures are configured separately so their behavior remains explicit.

TipTap 提供排除列表，你可以让选区弹窗远离敏感应用或需要高度专注的应用。高级手势单独配置，因此行为边界更明确。
