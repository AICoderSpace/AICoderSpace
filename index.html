TipTap

Select. Tap. Done.

选中。点击。完成。

Select any text on your Mac and a popup appears instantly — copy, search, translate, rewrite, summarize, open links, and more. Built on the idea that the best tool gets out of your way.

在 Mac 上选中任意文字，弹窗立刻出现——复制、搜索、翻译、改写、总结、打开链接，一触即发。核心理念：最好的工具，是不打扰你的工具。
